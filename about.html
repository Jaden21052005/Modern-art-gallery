About The Modern Frame

Founded in 2015, The Modern Frame has established itself as a premier destination for contemporary art in the heart of the city. Our mission is to bridge the gap between emerging artists and art enthusiasts, creating a space where creativity flourishes and meaningful connections are forged.

Located in a beautifully restored 19th-century building, our gallery spans three floors and features rotating exhibitions, permanent collections, and artist studios. We pride ourselves on discovering fresh talent while honoring established masters of contemporary art.

"Art is not what you see, but what you make others see." - Edgar Degas
